GAME RULES

Rules are pretty simple. If the you wins, you gain one point. If the computer wins, you loses one point. For example, if you have selected a stone and the computer selects scissors, you win. Below is a list of all possible combinations.

  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Rock beats Lizard
  • Lizard beats Spock
  • Spock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Lizard
  • Paper beats Spock
  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Lizard beats Paper
  • Spock beats Rock
